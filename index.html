A little bit about me

I started knitting when I was about 13 years old. Founder of this hobby in my family was my grandmother. She taught me everything from knitting a square to socks and gloves. In addition to that we had several knitting books and magazines that I read from cover to cover multiple times. When google and youtube started hitting off - finding information became even easier and I went wild! I started to look for more complicated patterns and stitches to try. I made close to a hundred of projects and now looking back, I am a little bit sad that I didn't document or at least pictured them. But anyway, there is still much more to try! In my knitting “career” I faced some obstacles and had numerous questions, how to do or even begin understanding patterns. I am here to share my knowledge and collected wisdom with you.

Knitting is one of my most relaxing and enjoyable hobbies. It helps to develop focus, clear your mind, sometimes even meditate and all while creating something valuable. I personally think that a knitted gift is a very heartwarming and caring gesture. But that is all about me, it is your turn now!
