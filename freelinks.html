Free knitting Patterns

There are a lot of pages that offer thousands of knitting patterns. While most of them require payment, there are those, which offer some less complicated projects for free.

Below you can find some links to pages, which offer free knitting patterns. I am sure that if you search the internet, you can find more of these kinds of pages. The ones that I am offering to visit are just my personal favourites.
Throughout my years off knitting and crocheting I used them numerous times!
  • My absolute favourite page, when I am looking for inspiration and free knitting patterns - Lovecrafts.com
  • A lot of really cute designs containing page: Yarnspirations.com
  • Also really often used in my search for a new project - Drops Design.
  • This one has some simple designs and even toys for kids. It is also a shop for knitting supplies: Hobbii.com
  • This page is the best if you are looking for something bright, cosy and modern. I personally used this one more than once to knit Baby Clothes Sets as gifts for my friends - Love Crafts.
  • Page that suggests 23 easy designs for beginners - The spruce Crafts.
